# Salaryman
### _Beneath the Surface, Inside the Hell_

Salaryman is a 3D Game with Isometric view set in the Hell. It is a simulation type game in which the player works on the gates of hell in place of Hades, as Hades is on a date with his wife. The player has to evaluate the souls sent to hell based on their bad and good deeds and send them to a particular level of hell or reconsideration to the ferryman. The souls will try to trick you by changing their name, make sure you dont get fooled and send them to where they really belong.

> The game was made in Unity, all the visual assets were made on Blender and each and every sound effect and background music was produced in FL Studio by us. No external/pre made assets were used.


And of course Salaryman is open source with a [public repository](https://github.com/HugeCabbage69/Salaryman) on GitHub.


#### Controls
| Key | Actions|
| --- | ---- |
| W | Moves character Forward |
| A | Moves character Left |
|S | To Moves character Backwards |
|D |Move right
|F | Interacts with the object|
|1-9| To send people to respective floor|

### Interactions
- Interact with the music box to play and pause the lo-fi music 🎵
- Interact with bell the to call the next soul 🛎️
- Enter the number on lift to select the floor of hell you want to send the soul to 🔢
